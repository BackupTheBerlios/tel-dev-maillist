Mutt alias files have the following structure:
alias <shortname> <email> (<fullname>)

The shortname can be created by combining firstname  and
lastname in a creative way, or by adding a new field called
"nickname" to the address file.

An example of mutt export can be found at
ftp://ftp.berlios.de/pub/tel in the mutt.pl script. Look at
the function "mutt_ex"
